# Worthlane V1 product principles

Every visible V1 feature must serve at least one of these rules:

1. **Simple**
2. **Reduces stress**
3. **Shows what’s next**

The V1 experience is manual-first. Bank sync, AI guidance, subscriptions, budgets,
goals, streaks, and advanced reporting remain outside the primary experience until
they can support these rules without adding friction or pressure.
